Module uploads are stored under this folder (see config/app.php: upload_history_dir).

Subfolders (examples):
  compliance/          — Compliance item evidence & documents
  reports/             — Reports module quick uploads
  circulars/           — Circular Intelligence PDFs/docs
  orgs/                — Organization logos
  bulk_compliance/     — Archived CSVs from Bulk Upload
  bulk_status/
  bulk_doa/
  bulk_matrix/
  bulk_ratios/
  financial_ratios/    — Financial Ratios CSV uploads (and bulk overlap may use bulk_ratios)
